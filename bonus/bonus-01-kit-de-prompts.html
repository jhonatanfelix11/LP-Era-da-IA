Bônus 4 — 15 Prompts de Vídeo para Higgsfield e Kling

Lembrete — prompts em inglês funcionam melhor

Todos os prompts abaixo estão em inglês. Para criar o seu em português: escreva a cena desejada para o Claude ou ChatGPT e peça "Traduza para um prompt de geração de vídeo em inglês, no estilo cinematográfico."

  • Produto cosmético: "Luxury perfume bottle on dark marble surface, slow rotating movement, soft golden rim light, macro lens, premium commercial style, 6 seconds"
  • Restaurante: "Chef hands carefully plating a gourmet dish, cinematic close-up, warm kitchen light, slow motion, Michelin star aesthetic"
  • Fitness: "Athletic person running on a misty mountain trail at sunrise, wide shot, camera tracking alongside, cinematic grading"
  • Tech/SaaS: "Hands typing on a sleek laptop with holographic data visualization floating above the screen, dark ambient office, blue accent light"
  • Moda: "Model walking in slow motion on a minimalist white studio, fabric flowing, high fashion editorial, side lighting"
  • Imóvel: "Luxury apartment interior, slow dolly forward through open-concept living room, golden hour light through floor-to-ceiling windows"
  • Carro: "Sports car driving on coastal road at sunset, low angle tracking shot, dramatic sky, cinematic color grading"
  • Abertura de vídeo: "Text title reveal: 'Era da I.A.' appearing letter by letter with light particle effect, dark background, electric blue glow"
  • Natureza/marca sustentável: "Water droplets falling on green leaves in slow motion, extreme macro, natural diffused light"
  • Fintech/consultoria: "Businessman walking confidently in modern glass office building lobby, upward camera angle, sharp professional suit"
  • E-commerce produto: "Product unboxing hands reveal — sleek white box opening slowly to reveal device, clean studio, 4K"
  • Redes sociais: "Vertical phone screen recording showing social media feed scrolling, modern home office background"
  • Saúde/clínica: "Doctor and patient in bright modern consultation room, warm and reassuring atmosphere, shallow depth of field"
  • Educação/curso: "Student taking notes by open window with natural light, focused, calm atmosphere, soft bokeh background"
  • Evento: "Aerial drone shot of outdoor corporate event, golden hour, crowd of professionals networking"

20 Prompts de Vídeo Prontos para Usar

Cada prompt inclui descrição da cena, ângulo de câmera e movimento. Use diretamente no Kling, Higgsfield, Runway ou Seedance 2.0.

  • Hero shot produto: product rotating slowly on marble surface, overhead bird's eye view, studio lighting, macro lens, 4K
  • Testemunho: woman speaking to camera, eye level, shallow depth of field, warm interior light, medium close-up
  • Revelação dramática: camera slowly dolly in from wide shot to extreme close-up, dramatic chiaroscuro lighting
  • Cena de lifestyle: person walking in city, low angle hero shot, golden hour, cinematic 24fps
  • Unboxing: hands opening product box, overhead top-down view, clean white background, natural light
  • Antes e depois: split screen, left side dark and cluttered, right side bright and organized, static camera
  • Nature b-roll: aerial drone shot over tropical forest, slow pan left, magic hour, wide angle
  • Workspace: over-the-shoulder view of person typing on laptop, soft window light, medium shot
  • Comida: macro close-up of food steaming, extreme close-up, soft backlight, slow motion
  • Tech product: smartphone floating, 360 rotation, dark background, neon accent lighting, ultra HD
  • Emotion: face of person reacting to good news, dutch angle slight tilt, natural expression, first-person POV
  • Cityscape: time-lapse of city at night, wide establishing shot, static tripod, 4K
  • Fashion editorial: model walking toward camera, low angle, wind effect, Vogue-style cinematic
  • Data/tech: holograms and data streams floating in dark room, rotating around subject, sci-fi look
  • Sport/energy: athlete in motion, freeze frame then slow-mo, side angle tracking shot, high energy
  • Real estate: interior walk-through, smooth steadicam from entrance to main room, wide angle, daylight
  • Storytelling: book opening with pages flying, macro + wide shot combination, vintage warm tones
  • Corporate: team in meeting room, over-the-shoulder medium shot, clean corporate environment
  • Skincare: liquid product drop in water, ultra slow motion, macro, studio clean white
  • CTA urgency: countdown clock ticking, extreme close-up, dramatic red lighting, fast zoom in
